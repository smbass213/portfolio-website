How to Name Cats for Adoption

Tips and Ideas





Why Names Matter

When seeking homes for shelter or rescue cats giving the cats catchy names can help them garner attention and find their future families.


Considerations

Some considerations when naming cats are as follows:

  • Are any of the cats bonded pairs?
  • What are the demographics of cat adopters in your area?
  • What demographics do your shelter's marketing efforts reach outside of your area? (Only applicable if your shelter allows out-of-area adoptions.)
  • What names will stand out from the other cats your shelter has available?

Popular Categories

We have listed some popular types of names for cats below. It is best to think about what would appeal to the demographics you are trying to reach for adoption when naming shelter cats. If you are naming multiple cats that you wish to adopt out together, such as a bonded pair or litter mates, it can be helpful to give them names that go together in theme and or style.

  • Fast food menu items
  • Punny names based off of pop culture or historical figures
  • Names of mythological figures or creatures
  • Old-fashioned or formal names
